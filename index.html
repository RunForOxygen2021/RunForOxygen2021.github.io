About Us.

At the Indus International schools, we try and instill the values of social contribution in all our students. In an endeavor to alleviate the human suffering caused by the pandemic, our students have organized a unique fundraiser event. Looking at the desperate situation in our country and the over-burdened health care, we realize there can be two approaches to contribute as citizens:

  1. Keeping ourselves healthy.
  2. Helping provide oxygen cylinders to those undergoing treatment.

Towards this cause, we are organizing a 48-hour virtual event, called “Run for Oxygen”. All funds raised through this event will be donated to registered organizations that supply oxygen cylinders to government hospitals.

Rules and Regulations.

Completion of the official entry form, including online registration, confirms the Participant’s agreement to abide by these Rules and Regulations.

  1. Please register for the event by filling in the details in the registration form (see above). Once you have submitted the form, you will receive a confirmation email. Please register by the 27st of May.
  2. After Registration, please make the payment at the donation link above. Registration is only considered complete once you receive a payment receipt.
  3. Perform any physical activity of your choice, preferably indoors, or with necessary precautions, outdoors, for a duration of 30 minutes or more by the 30st of May 2021.
  4. Record your activity as a short video (not more than 10 or 15 seconds long) or as pictures. This will be considered as a proof of your participation and completion of activity.
  5. Email the recording of your activity to us at runforoxygen@indusschoolhyd.com, by the 31st of May 2021. In the email, please make sure to include:
    • Your Name
    • The activity you've done
    • Video or pictures as attachments
  6. Send the email, and receive your online certificate.
