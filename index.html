About Us.

Indus International School has always been at the forefront of taking advantage of every opportunity to alleviate human suffering - be it helping migrant labourers ffed themselves, or assisting relief efforts during natural calamities. We at Indus have always advocated physical and emotional fitness to overcome challenges in life.

Looking at the desperate situation in our country and the over-burdened health care system, we realised that we could help by getting life-saving oxygen to those in desperate need, and encouraging others to get fit while we were at it, so that they too do not fall victim to the virus. The students of our Grade 11, therefore, started "Run For Oxygen" - an initiative to get people fit and get oxygen to the people who need it most.

Run For O2xygen - An initiative by Indus international Hyderabad

A fundraising initiative by Indus International Hyderabad to assist in the provision of lifesaving oxygen cylinders to COVID patients in need. For the people, by the people.
