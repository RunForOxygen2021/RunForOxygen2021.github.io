About Us.

At Indus International Schools, we spiritedly try and instill values of social contribution in our students. In an endeavor to alleviate the human suffering caused by the pandemic, our students are organizing an event to give hope to the hopeless. Looking at the desperate situation in our country and the over-burdened health care, we realize there can be two approaches to contribute as citizens:
1. Keeping ourselves healthy.
2. Help to provide oxygen cylinders to the needy

Towards this cause, we are organizing a 48-hour virtual event named “Run for Oxygen”. All amount raised through this event will be handed over to registered organizations that are supplying oxygen cylinders to government hospitals.

Run For O2xygen - An initiative by Indus international Hyderabad

An initiative by Indus International School Hyderabad, to assist in providing lifesaving oxygen cylinders to COVID patients - A ray of hope for the hopeless.
